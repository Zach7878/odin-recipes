Steak

This looks like a ribeye but I don't really know. I just searched up r/steak in images and this popped up so I think it looks good enough to use as an image

  • Ribeye
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Olive Oil
  1. Season ribeye with salt and pepper
  2. Heat up iron-cast pan on high heat
  3. Slather ribeye in olive oil
  4. Put ribeye on pan
  5. Flip every thirty seconds until it looks its going to get burnt
  6. Let it sit for 7 minutes
  7. Eat
